Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Mar 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Beacon ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our email processing application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using Beacon, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for Beacon, including:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Profile picture (if provided through authentication services)
  • Account login credentials

2.2 Email Content

To provide our email processing services, we access and analyze the content of your emails, including:

  • Email senders and recipients
  • Email subject lines
  • Email body content
  • Dates and times
  • Attachments (metadata only)

2.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with Beacon, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Access times
  • Pages viewed
  • Features used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Beacon's functionality
  • Process and analyze your emails to deliver our email management features
  • Send emails on your behalf when you use our email response features
  • Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your preferences
  • Respond to your requests and customer service needs
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Protect against unauthorized access and legal liability

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When we no longer need to process your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Third-Party Services

Beacon uses third-party services to support various functions:

  • Email Service Providers: To connect to your email accounts
  • AI and Natural Language Processing Services: To analyze email content
  • Authentication Services: To securely manage user access
  • Analytics Services: To improve our application

These third-party services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

6.1 Google Workspace API Data Sharing

When you grant Beacon access to your Google Workspace data (including Gmail), we only share this data with the following:

  • Essential Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including Render (cloud hosting), Cloudflare (custom domain), and Redis (caching). These providers are governed by their respective standard terms of service, which include data protection provisions.
  • AI Processing Services: We use OpenAI's API for processing email content. This processing is done according to OpenAI's standard terms of service, which allow them to use API data to improve their services, though we only send the minimum data necessary for our features to function (mainly, the email content and metadata).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your Google Workspace data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties for their marketing purposes.

6.2 Gmail API Permissions

Our application requests the following Gmail API permissions:

  • Read access to your emails: To analyze and organize your inbox
  • Send emails on your behalf: To allow you to respond to emails directly from our application

When you use our email response features, we may send emails via the Gmail API, which makes the emails appear as being sent directly from your Gmail account. This ensures emails you send appear in your Gmail "Sent" folder and maintain the conversation thread continuity.

6.3 AI Model Training Disclosure

Beacon does not use or retain user data obtained through Google Workspace APIs to develop, improve, or train generalized AI and/or machine learning models. Any processing of email content for email management features is performed on a per-user basis and solely for the purpose of providing the service to that specific user.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction or objection to our processing of your personal information
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

8. Children's Privacy

Beacon is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:
